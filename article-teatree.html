Harnessing the Antibacterial Magic of Tea Tree Oil

While modern science provides the precision, nature provides the power. Discover why Tea Tree Oil is at the heart of the LaserTen formula.

An Ancient Antiseptic

Indigenous Australians have used Tea Tree (Melaleuca alternifolia) for thousands of years. Its unique combination of terpinen-4-ol makes it a broad-spectrum antimicrobial agent that is remarkably gentle on human tissue.

Terpinen-4-ol Mechanism

This compound increases the permeability of bacterial cell membranes, effectively causing them to leak and perish without harming the healthy microbiome of the mouth.

The Plaque Connection

Plaque is essentially a bacterial housing development. Tea Tree oil has a natural ability to disrupt these housing projects. It prevents bacteria from adhering to the tooth surface, making it much easier to rinse away before it hardens into tartar.

Safe & Effective

Unlike harsh alcohols used in many mouthwashes, Tea Tree Oil doesn't dry out your mouth. This is critical because a dry mouth is more prone to bacterial growth.
