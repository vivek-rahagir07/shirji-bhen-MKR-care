Why Single-Use Sachets are the New Standard for Travel

In an increasingly mobile world, carrying a bulky bottle of mouthwash or a messy tube of paste is outdated. We examine the rise of the hygiene sachet.

Purity in Every Pour

Traditional bottles are opened and closed dozens of times, exposing the contents to air and potential contaminants. LaserTen sachets are hermetically sealed until the moment you use them. This ensures the clinical potency of every dose is identical to the day it was manufactured.

Sustainable Innovation

By providing the perfect dose, we eliminate product waste. You use 100% of what's inside, with no residue left at the bottom of a plastic bottle. Our sachets are designed for minimal material usage while providing maximum protection.

Potency Retention

Traditional bottles lose up to 15% of their chemical efficacy within 3 months of opening due to oxygen exposure. Sachets isolate the formula, maintaining 100% efficacy for the entire shelf life.
